Currency Exchange Risk

The change in the exchange rate on the FOREX market is the basis for making a profit since a trader earns only when there is a price movement up or down. However, the change in the currency exchange rate is also the main risk that can lead to losing all the funds invested in trading. The exchange rate on the FOREX market is highly volatile and can be influenced by hundreds of different factors, and predicting their impact is not always possible. In fact, it is absolutely impossible to predict the impact of all factors.

Despite the volatility of currency movements, protective orders (such as stop-loss) can significantly reduce the risks associated with sudden changes in the direction of currency movements.
